## Administer Your Storefront with Business Manager
Business Manager provides tools for admins/devs to manage and customize ecommerce sites (site config, content, security, code deployment, etc.). Key features/functions listed:

- **Site configuration**: global + site-specific preferences (locale, currency, time zone); custom site preferences; supported locales/currencies; tax rules/configs.
- **User and role management**: roles/permissions; user accounts and access levels.
- **Security and access control**: password policies, IP allowlisting.
- **Search preferences**: indexing, synonyms, stop words; refinements to help filtering.
- **Localization settings**: support multiple languages/regions; import/export translation files.
- **Analytics and reporting**: dashboards + detailed reports for performance, sales, customer behavior, inventory, campaign effectiveness.
- **Code deployment**: manage/deploy cartridges (scripts/templates/resources); version control for safe updates/rollbacks.
- **Integration management**: web services + data feeds for import/export between storefront and external systems. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))

The unit emphasizes Business Manager as vital for setting up/configuring/managing storefronts effectively. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))

## Personalize the Shopper Experience with Agentforce Commerce for B2C Merchandising Tools
The unit groups merchandising capabilities:

### Product and Catalog Management
- **Catalogs, categories, products**: manage properties associated with catalogs, categories, price books, and navigation to control browsing/engagement.
- **Product detail pages**: enrich PDPs with image displays, videos, detailed descriptions; bulk import or add products individually. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))

### Content and Storefront Design
- **Page Designer**: design/schedule/publish custom pages without code; drag-and-drop reusable component types; create dynamic templates for product list or PDP pages that auto-populate inventory data.
- **Content slot configurations**: configure areas (content slots) to show products/categories/HTML; apply rules to control what appears to specific visitors (example: “Happy Birthday” banner only for qualifying users). ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))

### Marketing, Promotions, and Segmentation
- **Campaigns and promotions**: campaigns wrap experiences including promotions and content slot configs; define schedules + qualifiers (coupons, source codes) to control eligibility.
- **Dynamic customer groups**: integrate with Data 360 to segment customers in real time (profile data, session behavior, purchase history); target promotions to segments like loyalty shoppers or visitors from a region. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))

### Search and Navigation (Searchandising)
- **Sorting rules**: rank search results using active data metrics (“searchandising”) to prioritize top-rated items or move low-stock seasonal items to clearance.
- **Store-level refinement**: include local store inventory in results to support immediate pickup discovery. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))

### AI and Personalization Capabilities
- **Einstein search**: power recommendations; use predictive sort to personalize suggestions and ordering based on shopper behavior.
- **Agentforce Guided Shopping**: deploy agents to help shoppers find products and answer FAQs.
- **Agentforce Commerce insights**: analytics to identify purchasing trends; make inventory/marketing decisions; generate promotions based on insights. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))

### Omni-Channel and Social Integration
- **Fulfillment options**: connect shoppers with physical store inventory and fulfillment methods (real-time inventory for in-store/curbside pickup).
- **Social media integration**: integrate third-party social apps to personalize advertising and gain insights. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))

### URL Management
- **URL redirects**: manage redirects to maintain positive UX and preserve SEO ranking when URLs change.
- **SEO-related settings**: meta tags and canonical URLs to improve search visibility. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/cc-digital/learn-about-digital-features))
