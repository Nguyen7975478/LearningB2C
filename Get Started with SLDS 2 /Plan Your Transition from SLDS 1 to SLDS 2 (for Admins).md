## Learning Objectives
After completing this unit, you’ll be able to:
- Assess customization and branding readiness for SLDS 2.
- Prepare your org and users for enabling Cosmos.
- Roll out, support, and monitor the transition from SLDS 1 to SLDS 2. ([trailhead.salesforce.com](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/content/learn/modules/salesforce-lightning-design-system-2-for-admins/plan-your-transition-from-slds-1-to-slds-2?trail_id=get-started-with-slds-2))

---

## Assess Your Org
- Inventory all branding/custom configs (colors, logos, themes).
- Check for custom Lightning components or third-party packages styled directly using SLDS 1 or custom CSS. These may need validation/updating for SLDS 2/Cosmos support.
- Review accessibility settings; note any UI workarounds that may not be needed with SLDS 2.

---

## Prepare for Enablement
- Create a backup of theme/config settings.
- Set up a test/sandbox org. Enable Cosmos in Theme settings (Setup → Themes & Branding).
- Review your org’s Lightning Pages, apps, and custom components for look-and-feel with Cosmos enabled.
- Communicate upcoming visual changes and branding impact to leadership and end users.
- Train super users/admins on the new Theme Editor and accessibility best practices.

---

## Roll Out the New Look
- Activate Cosmos theme org-wide in production.
- Encourage users to give feedback. Provide quick guides or in-app prompts as needed.
- Do incremental rollout to pilot groups before enabling for all (optional for larger orgs).

---

## Support Users and Monitor Feedback
- Monitor for issues with custom branding, custom components, or partner-built apps.
- Provide a feedback channel (e.g., Chatter group or Trailblazer Community).
- Work with devs/partners to remediate styling issues or leverage Salesforce support as needed.
- Iterate on theme customizations based on user feedback.

### Đánh giá org
- Kiểm tra tất cả branding/custom theme (color, logo, theme).
- Review Lightning component/custom CSS dùng SLDS 1—cần validate, có thể phải chỉnh lại cho SLDS 2.
- Review accessibility; ghi lại workaround UI có thể bỏ khi lên SLDS 2.

---

### Chuẩn bị bật Cosmos
- Backup theme/config.
- Tạo sandbox thử nghiệm; bật Cosmos (Setup > Themes & Branding).
- Xem trước Lightning Page/App/Component khi có Cosmos.
- Báo trước thay đổi tới leader/user; hướng dẫn người dùng ưu tú về Theme Editor và accessibility.

---

### Rollout new look
- Bật Cosmos trên production.
- Nhận feedback.
- Rollout thử cho pilot group trước (với org to).

---

### Hỗ trợ & nhận feedback
- Theo dõi bug/custom branding/component/partner app không tương thích.
- Tạo group feedback (Chatter/Trailblazer).
- Cùng dev/partner fix issues; tham khảo Salesforce support nếu cần.
- Chỉnh sửa theme theo feedback.
