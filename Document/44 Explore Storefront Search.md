### Storefront search fundamentals (supported by the other units in this module)
- Search behavior is controlled by **Search Preferences** (availability filtering, autocorrections, Einstein features, redirect keyword rules, index notifications).
- Guided search is implemented via **Search Driven Redirects** and evaluated by **SearchRedirectURL pipelet** before standard search processing.
- Findability and relevance depend on configuring **Searchable Attributes** (product/category/content attributes + boost factor + autocorrect decisions) and rebuilding the relevant index.
