## English

## Learning Objectives
- List three ways to guarantee a campaign’s success.
- List the four experiences you can configure in a campaign.
- Describe the three promotion classes.
- Explain how qualifiers work relative to promotions and campaigns.

## What merchandisers plan to guarantee campaign success
When creating a campaign, merchandisers determine (unit list):
- Audience
- Marketing sources (affiliates, email)
- Site promotion locations (homepage, category pages, checkout)
- Experiences
- Qualifiers
- Products or categories in each promotion
- Schedules

## What a B2C Commerce campaign is (ground rules)
- A B2C Commerce **campaign** contains scheduled experiences that influence a specific set of shoppers.
- Experience types can include promotions and other merchandising controls.
- Shoppers must qualify for an experience.
- Experiences run within the campaign’s overall schedule.
- A campaign is defined in the context of a **site** and isn’t shared among sites.
- A disabled campaign is inactive and not available to shoppers.

## Experience types (4) you can configure in a campaign
The unit lists:
1) **Promotions**
2) **Content slots**
3) **Sorting rules**
4) **Keyword search sorting rules**

### Content slots (how they fit)
- A content slot can be part of a campaign (for single-view scheduling with promotions), but content slots can also be configured outside campaigns.

### Sorting rules and keyword search sorting rule
- Sorting rules can be used as campaign experiences (for example: show clearance first).
- Keyword search sorting rules can also be campaign experiences (sorting behavior tied to certain keyword searches).
- Sorting rules can exist outside campaigns too; campaigns are not required to use them.

## Promotions (promotion classes)
A B2C Commerce **promotion** is a set of rules that define how and when a shopper gets a discount, and the discount details.
The three promotion classes are:
- **Product promotion**
- **Order promotion**
- **Shipping promotion**

## What a qualifier is (and how it relates)
- **Qualifiers** trigger a promotion and can be:
    - **Coupons**
    - **Customer groups**
    - **Source codes**
- You can set multiple qualifiers for a promotion (example: must be a loyalty member AND enter a coupon code).
- When coupons/source codes/customer groups are used in campaigns, they define which shoppers qualify for experiences.

## Build a playbook (campaign planning artifact)
The unit’s playbook concept captures:
- Campaign schedule (dates/times)
- Required assets (photos, labels, content)
- Promotions (with their own schedules)
- Products/categories included
- Audience segments and timing
- Marketing sources and timing
- Messaging locations (homepage/cart/etc.)

## Recommended process (efficient workflow)
Instead of “create campaign then stop to create qualifiers then promotions”, the unit recommends:
1) Create the qualifiers
2) Create the experiences
3) Create the campaign

- Experiences
- Qualifiers
- Products hoặc categories trong mỗi promotion

## Campaign là gì (ground rules)
- **Campaign** chứa scheduled experiences ảnh hưởng tới một nhóm shoppers cụ thể.
- Shoppers phải qualify thì mới nhận experience.
- Experiences phải chạy trong overall schedule của campaign.
- Campaign thuộc một **site** (không shared giữa sites).
- Campaign bị disabled thì inactive và shoppers không thấy/không áp dụng.

## 4 experience types trong campaign
1) **Promotions**
2) **Content slots**
3) **Sorting rules**
4) **Keyword search sorting rules**

### Content slots
- Có thể đưa content slot vào campaign để quản lý theo lịch “single view”, nhưng content slots cũng có thể cấu hình ngoài campaign.

### Sorting rules và keyword search sorting rule
- Sorting rules có thể là experience trong campaign (ví dụ show clearance first).
- Keyword search sorting rules cũng có thể là experience trong campaign (sorting gắn với keyword searches).
- Cả hai cũng có thể tồn tại ngoài campaign.

## Promotions (promotion classes)
**Promotion** là tập rules xác định khi nào shopper nhận discount và discount đó là gì.
3 promotion classes:
- **Product promotion**
- **Order promotion**
- **Shipping promotion**

## Qualifier là gì và liên quan thế nào
- **Qualifiers** trigger promotion, gồm:
    - **Coupons**
    - **Customer groups**
    - **Source codes**
- Có thể gán nhiều qualifiers cho 1 promotion (ví dụ loyalty member AND coupon code).
- Khi dùng trong campaign, qualifiers quyết định shopper nào qualify cho experiences.

## Build a playbook (tài liệu kế hoạch)
Playbook trong unit bao gồm:
- Campaign schedule
- Required assets (photos/labels/content)
- Promotions (kèm schedule riêng)
- Products/categories
- Audience + timing
- Marketing sources + timing
- Messaging locations

## Quy trình khuyến nghị (workflow hiệu quả)
Unit khuyến nghị:
1) Create the qualifiers
2) Create the experiences
3) Create the campaign
